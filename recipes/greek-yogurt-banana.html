Banana Greek Yogurt

Description

Greek yogurt is a quick and easy snack. It is high in protein and low in fat and carbohydrates. This powerful combination allows for a generous portion without fear of consuming a monstrous amount of calories. Tossing in a banana makes for a delicious treat.

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt of choice
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Scoop the desired amount of greek yogurt into a bowl
  2. Cut up a banana
  3. Place the cut up banana into the bowl with the greek yogurt
  4. Enjoy!
